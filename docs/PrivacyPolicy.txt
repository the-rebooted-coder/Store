Memories App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30/08/2023
Thank you for choosing Memories, the app designed to help you monitor your device's battery status. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your privacy while using Memories.

1. Introduction:
Memories is a battery monitoring app dedicated to providing you with timely alerts when your phone's battery level exceeds 85%. We respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect:
Memories is designed to be a minimalistic and privacy-conscious app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data or information. We want to assure you that:

We do not collect or access any personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address.
We do not track your location or access your device's GPS.
We do not collect data about how you use the app, including the pages you visit or the features you interact with.
We do not access your device's contact list or collect any information about your contacts.

3. Alerts and Battery Monitoring:
Memories's primary function is to monitor your device's battery status and provide alerts when it reaches or exceeds 85%. This is done solely for the purpose of enhancing your device's battery management, and we do not retain any historical data or information related to your battery usage.

4. Third-Party Services:
Memories does not integrate with any third-party services, including social media platforms or analytics tools. We do not share your battery status or any other data with external organizations.

5. Data Security:
We take your privacy seriously, and even though we do not collect data, we have implemented security measures to protect any app-related data. These measures include encryption and regular app updates to maintain a high level of security.

6. Contact Us:
If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy while using Memories, please do not hesitate to contact us at connectwithspandan@gmail.com

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy:
Memories may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

By using Memories, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.
Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we are committed to maintaining the trust you place in us. Thank you for using Memories.
